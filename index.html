Grand Opening: Stanford

Tompkins Tennis is excited to announce its partnership with Brandon Coupe, one of the men's tennis coaches for Stanford University, to run clinics on campus. The program will be called "BCT Training." We are hosting a grand opening event on Sunday, February 10th . 10:00 a.m.-1:00 p.m. Price is $20 per person. The event includes junior and adult on-court tennis clinics along with an exhibition match. Breakfast included. To RSVP, please email sandy@tompkinssport.com

Katie Volynets Wins Girls 16 Orange Bowl International & Eddie Herr International!

Congratulations to Katie Volynets for winning the Girls 16 Orange Bowl! She defeated Imani Graham in the finals by the score of 6-4, 6-3. Katie had to overcome a six hour rain delay before finally taking the court. Katie made history by becoming the first girl to win both the Girls 16 Eddie Herr and Orange Bowl in the same year.

Currently Katie is ranked #1 Nationally in Girls 16s.
