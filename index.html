Programs

  Pebble Beach Camp

From June 19 to July 29th, 2016, Tompkins Tennis International runs an exclusive summer camp in beautiful Pebble Beach, CA for beginning players to Nationally Ranked Juniors. The most fun your child will have all summer!

Ranked Juniors

Designed for top players with a minimum USTA Norcal ranking of 100 in their respective age groups. An intense mentally and physically challenging program. Try-out required before enrollment.

Training Program II

This program is geared toward the younger junior training year-round, practicing five days per week, and competing in the USTA 10's and 12's age divisions. This is an invite only program and a try-out is required prior to registration.
