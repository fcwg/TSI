Sunday
May 22, 2016, 2 PM

World Renowned Sports Psychologist

Jeff Greenwald

Complimentary Lecture

At Livermore Valley Tennis Club




Sports psychology consultant and author, Jeff Greenwald, will be facing off with a highly ranked Norcal player in a competitive exhibition match demonstrating the mental skills and decision-making of the best players in the world.

Formerly ranked No. 1 in the world by the ITF in his age group and a two-time senior world-champion, Jeff will show you how to manage your mind and body in the context of high level tennis in real time. You will have the opportunity to be on-court, ask your most burning questions about competition and the mental game and talk with Jeff. He will also be holding a book signing after the event so your son or daughter can meet him personally and get an autographed book. Don’t miss this event!



Bio: Jeff Greenwald is an internationally recognized sports psychology consultant and author of Amazon’s best-selling book, The Best Tennis of Your Life, and audio program, Fearless Tennis. He has appeared in The New York Times, USA Today, Tennis Magazine and numerous podcasts and radio shows around the U.S and has been a consultant for the USTA for the past ten years. Jeff is passionate about helping juniors learn these critical mental skills earlier than he did. He lives in Marin with his wife and two children, both of whom play junior tennis.
